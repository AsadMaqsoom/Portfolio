About Me

My Introduction

I am a passionate web developer with a fresh yet solid foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies. I recently completed a comprehensive web development course at APTECH, where I honed my skills in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, MySQL, and PHP. Additionally, I have worked extensively with various libraries to enhance my development projects.

I hold a Master's degree in Geography from Karachi University. For the past three years, I have been working as a freelancer in the field of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and Remote Sensing (RS). GIS involves the analysis and visualization of spatial data to understand patterns and relationships, while RS uses satellite imagery to monitor and assess environmental changes. My work in this field has equipped me with strong analytical skills and a keen eye for detail.

Combining my expertise in web development with my background in GIS and RS, I am eager to create innovative and dynamic web applications that address real-world challenges. I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest industry trends to deliver high-quality solutions.
